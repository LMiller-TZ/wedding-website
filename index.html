Why Texas?

We went through our guest list and found out 75% or more of our guests would have to travel over multiple state lines regardless of where our wedding was because of how spread out our friends and family are. So we wanted to pick a venue that would make your travel & time worth it! Here are a few reasons why you will want to come for the entire weekend:

  • Open bars all weekend!
  • Stay in a house on the property, no need to worry about driving/Ubering after a night of partying!
  • Lake activites - Blob, canoeing, kayaking, stand up paddle boarding, sand volleyball, rope swings, waterslide, beach (yes, nonstop beach offs)
  • Sporting activites - Zip Line over the lake, 18-hole frisbee golf course, skeet shooting, axe throwing, archery, baseball diamond, pickleball, gaga ball, basketball, tennis courts, fishing
  • Miles of hiking, jogging, and biking trails
  • Live Band Karaoke
  • Cabin Wars
  • Meals included in stay

The only things you should have to pay for the entire trip is your flight, your transportation to the venue, and your room. We have everything else covered!

Accommodations

Balcones Springs offers cozy cabins & bungalows grouped together across the property. Think glamping. Each cabin is uniquely decorated and all feature TempurPedic mattress toppers, adjustable A/C and heating, refrigerators, toiletries, WiFi access points, and en-suite bathroom. The cabins can sleep 4-6 guests in twins or king size beds.

How we met

We first met on a cruise back in 2013. On the first day of the cruise I, Luke, nervously walked into the Teens Club and was immediately greeted by Ali shouting "HEYYY" the second I walked in. Who would've thought the first person I talked to on that ship and the one who made me feel comfortable in a new, awkward environment would be the person I would fall in love with. Well... not me, at least not right away. It wasn't until halfway through the cruise where we played Just Dance 2013 together and I instantly felt a chemistry together unlike any I had before. I contintued to be oblivious to all the signals she was sending but eventually asked if she "wanted to be official?"... And the rest was history.
